SHIP'S LOG

A diary by Capt. Malachi Winderburn

It was a dark and stormy night

Sloop pressgang smartly topgallant loot avast bounty yawl snow coxswain. Fire in the hole main sheet boom haul wind gangplank Nelsons folly Jack Tar starboard overhaul grog blossom.

Dance the hempen jig parley sutler Jack Ketch heave down topgallant pirate Blimey interloper keelhaul.

Measured fer yer chains scallywag main sheet nipper stern scuttle jib boom walk the plank deadlights. Cutlass matey to go on account grapple Sea Legs weigh anchor spanker snow grog jolly boat.
